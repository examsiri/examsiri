Andhra Pradesh State Board

The Directorate of Government Examinations or the Andhra Pradesh Board of Secondary Education is the main education Board in the state of Andhra Pradesh. The Andhra Pradesh Board of Secondary Education (BSEAP), which is also known as the AP SSC Board conducts the Class 10 board exams in the state. And the Board of Intermediate Education (BIE) conducts the Andhra Intermediate or Class 12 exams. In addition to this, the AP Board is also responsible for prescribing the syllabus, curriculum and textbooks for Class 1 to Class 12 students in the state. Another important function of the AP Board is to evaluate the Class 10 and Class 12 Board exam and announce the results.

Assam State Board

Assam State Board is a state-level education board responsible for conducting secondary (class 10) and higher secondary (class 12) examinations in the state of Assam, India. The board was established in the year 1962 and is headquartered in Guwahati. The Assam State Board of Secondary Education (SEBA) conducts the class 10 board exams, while the Assam Higher Secondary Education Council (AHSEC) conducts the class 12 board exams.
The board is responsible for prescribing the syllabus and textbooks for the schools affiliated with it, conducting exams, publishing results, and granting recognition to schools in the state. The board also conducts various other examinations and provides certifications such as the Teachers' Eligibility Test (TET) for the recruitment of teachers in the state.
