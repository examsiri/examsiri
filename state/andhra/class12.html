Andhra Pradesh State Board Class 12 Exam

The Andhra Pradesh State Board conducts the Class 12 board exam annually in March for students in Andhra Pradesh. This exam is considered a significant milestone for students as it determines their future academic and professional opportunities. The Board sets the exam papers, conducts the exams, and declares the results. The exam comprises various subjects such as Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, English, and other language papers. Students are advised to study diligently and prepare well for the exams to achieve their desired results. Students can also refer to previous year papers to understand the exam pattern and difficulty level of the exam.

Subject List :

The subjects in each group for the AP Class 12 Exam are as follows:

  • Group A: English, Telugu/Hindi/Sanskrit/Urdu, and a third language chosen from Telugu/Hindi/Sanskrit/Urdu/Arabic/French
  • Group B: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology, Commerce, Economics, Accountancy, Civics, History, Geography, and Sociology

The Andhra Pradesh Class 12 exam, also known as the Intermediate Public Examination (IPE), is a crucial academic milestone for students in the state. It is conducted by the Andhra Pradesh Board of Intermediate Education (APBIE) and serves as a gateway to higher education and career opportunities.

Pattern of the Andhra Pradesh Class 12 Exam:

The AP Class 12 Exam consists of two years - 1st Year and 2nd Year. Each year consists of two groups: Group A and Group B. Each group has three subjects, making a total of six subjects in each year. The exam is conducted in the following three streams:

  • Science (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology)
  • Commerce (Economics, Commerce, Accountancy, Mathematics)
  • Humanities (Economics, History, Civics, Geography) 

Preparation Tips:

Here are some tips to help students prepare for the AP Class 12 Exam:

  • Create a study schedule and stick to it
  • Understand the exam pattern and syllabus thoroughly
  • Practice previous years' question papers and sample papers
  • Take frequent breaks and stay physically and mentally healthy
  • Revise regularly and focus on weaker areas
  • Seek guidance from teachers and mentors, and clarify doubts

In conclusion, the Andhra Pradesh Class 12 Exam is a crucial step towards achieving academic and career goals. By following the exam pattern, preparing for the subjects, and adhering to the schedule, students can score well and secure their future.
