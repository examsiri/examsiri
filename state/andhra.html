Andhra Pradesh State Board

The Directorate of Government Examinations or the Andhra Pradesh Board of Secondary Education is the main education Board in the state of Andhra Pradesh. The Andhra Pradesh Board of Secondary Education (BSEAP), which is also known as the AP SSC Board conducts the Class 10 board exams in the state. And the Board of Intermediate Education (BIE) conducts the Andhra Intermediate or Class 12 exams. In addition to this, the AP Board is also responsible for prescribing the syllabus, curriculum and textbooks for Class 1 to Class 12 students in the state. Another important function of the AP Board is to evaluate the Class 10 and Class 12 Board exam and announce the results.

Andhra Pradesh State Board Class 10 Exam

The Andhra Pradesh State Board conducts the Class 10 board exam annually in March for students in Andhra Pradesh. This exam is also known as the Secondary School Certificate (SSC) exam and is considered a crucial exam for students as it determines their future academic and professional opportunities. The Board sets the exam papers, conducts the exams, and declares the results. The exam comprises various subjects such as Mathematics, Science, Social Science, English, and other language papers. Students are advised to study diligently and prepare well for the exams to achieve their desired results. Students can also refer to previous year papers to understand the exam pattern and difficulty level of the exam.
