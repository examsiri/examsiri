Jammu & Kashmir State Board

The Jammu and Kashmir State Board of School Education (JKBOSE) is responsible for conducting the Class 10, Class 11, and Class 12 board exams in the state of Jammu and Kashmir. The JKBOSE is an autonomous body under the administration of the state government of Jammu and Kashmir.

Jammu & Kashmir State Board Class 10 Exam

The Class 10 exam conducted by the JKBOSE is known as the Secondary School Examination (SSE). The exam is usually held in the month of March and April. The SSE exam is an important milestone in the academic life of a student as it paves the way for their future academic pursuits. The SSE exam consists of various subjects including English, Mathematics, Science, Social Science, and more. Students who qualify the SSE exam are eligible to apply for higher secondary education in any stream of their choice.

Jammu & Kashmir State Board Class 11 Exam

The Class 11 exam conducted by the JKBOSE is known as the Higher Secondary Part-1 (HSP-1) exam. The exam is usually held in the month of November and December. The HSP-1 exam is an important step in a student's academic journey as it sets the foundation for their future academic pursuits. The HSP-1 exam consists of various subjects including English, Mathematics, Science, Commerce, and more. Students who qualify the HSP-1 exam are eligible to apply for higher secondary education in any stream of their choice.

Jammu & Kashmir State Board Class 12 Exam

The Class 12 exam conducted by the JKBOSE is known as the Higher Secondary Part-2 (HSP-2) exam. The exam is usually held in the month of March and April. The HSP-2 exam is a crucial milestone in a student's academic life as it determines their eligibility for undergraduate courses in various fields of study. The HSP-2 exam consists of various subjects including English, Mathematics, Science, Commerce, and more. Students who qualify the HSP-2 exam are eligible to apply for undergraduate courses in any field of their choice.
