Kerala State Board

Kerala state board exams are conducted for Class 10, Class 11, and Class 12 students. These exams play a crucial role in a student's academic journey and are an important factor in determining their future educational and career paths. In this article, we will take a closer look at each of these exams and provide useful tips and strategies to help students prepare for them.

Kerala State Board Class 10 Exam

Kerala State Board conducts Class 10 examinations annually for students studying in secondary school. The board offers various subjects including mathematics, science, social science, and languages such as English, Malayalam, and Hindi. The Class 10 exam pattern consists of both objective and subjective type questions, and the total marks are usually 500.

Kerala State Board Class 11 Exam

Kerala State Board conducts Class 11 examinations annually for students studying in higher secondary school. The board offers various streams such as science, commerce, and arts, and students can choose subjects accordingly. The Class 11 exam pattern consists of both objective and subjective type questions, and the total marks are usually 600.

Kerala State Board Class 12 Exam

Kerala State Board conducts Class 12 examinations annually for students studying in higher secondary school. The board offers various streams such as science, commerce, and arts, and students can choose subjects accordingly. The Class 12 exam pattern consists of both objective and subjective type questions, and the total marks are usually 1200.
